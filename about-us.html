About us

Ah Hua Kelong is a Fish Farm located off changi and sembawang. We specializes in farming Grouper, Seabass and Golden pomfret. We also harvest Flower Crabs and Extra large mussels. Our distribution channel deals direct to restaurants, intermediaries and we are taking a big step to deliver small orders to residences.

We are keeping good track records of providing high quality to satisfied customers because we take pride in doing what we do best, Farming.
